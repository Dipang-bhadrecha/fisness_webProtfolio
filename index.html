About Fisness

Bringing the Fishing Industry into the Digital Age

Fisness is a full-stack fisheries management platform tailored specifically for the Gujarat fishing community. It bridges the gap between traditional fishing operations and modern digital management — making record-keeping, crew coordination, and financial tracking accessible to everyone from small-boat fishermen to large vessel owners.

Built with a deep understanding of the local context, Fisness supports Gujarati language and workflows that match how fishing businesses actually operate — from tali (weight ledgers) to kharchi (expenses) and beyond.

Core Features

Everything a Fishing Operation Needs

From the dock to the office — Fisness gives every stakeholder the tools they need to operate efficiently.

⚓
Vessel & Fleet Management

Track every boat in your fleet — status (at sea, docked, under repair), assignment history, and boat-level expenses. Get a live overview of your entire fleet from a single dashboard.

📋
Tali & Catch Logging

Record daily fish landings, species breakdown, and weight measurements digitally. Generate tali bills instantly and share them with buyers — eliminating paper-based errors and disputes.

👥
Crew Management & Payroll

Manage crew rosters, assign members to trips, and track individual earnings. Automate pay calculations based on catch share or fixed-rate agreements — reducing end-of-trip settlement disputes.

💰
Kharchi & Financial Tracking

Log trip expenses (fuel, ice, repairs, provisions), categorize costs, and generate profit & loss reports per trip or per vessel. Keep your books accurate without an accountant on board.

Tech Stack

Modern, Production-Grade Architecture

Fisness is built on a reliable, scalable stack designed for real-world mobile and backend demands.

Mobile App
React Native + Expo

Cross-platform iOS & Android app built with Expo Router, typed with TypeScript, and using Zustand for state management.

Backend API
Node.js + Fastify

High-performance REST API server with Prisma ORM, JWT authentication, and role-based permission middleware.

Database
PostgreSQL

Relational database managed via Prisma ORM with full migration support, Docker Compose for local development, and structured schema for multi-tenant fisheries data.
